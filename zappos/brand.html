Before I began developing this site, I created a style tile to help guide my design for the site. I wanted to keep it fun and inject the personality I believe that Zappos does such a great job portraying. I chose a color scheme, font pairing, and adjectives to help guide my development. I always try to begin my developent with a style guide because I truly believe a good site is driven by good design.

I know frameworks such as Bootstrap are usually the way to go but I've been trying to stray away from that because I think it can be limiting. I've realized that there certain layouts I like better, and perhaps down the line I'll create my own framwork.

Tools used: Sass, flexbox, Toast
